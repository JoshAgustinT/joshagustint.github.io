About

Hello again! In case you missed it, my name is Joshua. I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at the University of Utah, where I also earned my Bachelor’s degree in 2024. My most most extensive background is in computer systems, and I’ve taken graduate-level courses in compilers, operating systems, computer architecture and interactive computer graphics. I have extensive experience with C++ in performance-critical applications. Still, I also enjoy using JavaScript to build interactive web applications and mixing in WebGL whenever I can, as well as using Python for personal scripting projects and quick experiments. Graduate school has allowed me to dive deep into systems research, explore how computers work at a fundamental level, find ways to make them faster or safer, and learn from the process along the way. Working in a team of researchers has been one of the most rewarding parts of that experience, from the discussions that spark new ideas to the sense of shared progress. I plan to continue my research in systems and pursue a PhD focused on building computing systems that are both efficient and secure.

Joshua Tlatelpa-Agustin
