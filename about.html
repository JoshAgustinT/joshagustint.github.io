About

Hello again! In case you missed my name, I’m Joshua. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Utah in 2024. My strongest background is in systems, and I’ve taken graduate-level courses in Compilers, Operating Systems, and Interactive Computer Graphics. I have extensive experience with C++ (still somehow not great at it though), particularly in performance-critical applications, but I’m also proficient in a wide range of popular programming languages. Additionally, I enjoy using JavaScript to create interactive web applications, Python for personal scripting projects, and WebGL for developing interactive 3D web apps. Beyond technical skills, I value structured problem-solving and have a strong interest in both the theoretical and practical aspects of computer systems. I’m excited to continue my studies and research in systems. My ultimate goal is to continue my graduate studies at the University of Utah and eventually earn a PhD in Computer Science.
